SPECIAL MECHANICS

Expect the unexpected

Modifiers that change the rules mid-pour. Learn them. Master them.

Reverse Start

Gravity flips for the first 6 seconds. What falls up must come down — time your release.

Crosswind

A gentle lateral oscillation pushes your stream left and right in a slow sine wave.

Gravity Surge

Every 7 seconds, gravity spikes to 2.2×. Predict the surge — or watch your liquid miss the hole.

Shrinking Hole

The target radius shrinks over time. Fill it fast before it becomes too small to hit.

Moving Hole

The target sways horizontally in a smooth arc. Lead the hole, not where it is.

Vortex

A spinning force field above the hole curves every particle's trajectory. Aim wide, aim smart.

Wind Gusts

Sudden lateral blasts every 4 seconds disrupt your stream without warning. Stay adaptive.
